How to Crochet the Jack and Sally Scarves

Materials Needed

  1. Yarn: Choose black, white, and your favorite accent color.
  2. Crochet hook: Size G (4.00mm) or as recommended by the yarn label.
  3. Tapestry needle: Used for weaving in ends.

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Start by chaining a desired length for your scarf. It's best to measure around your neck for a comfortable fit.
  2. Row 1: Single crochet in the second chain from the hook and in each chain across. Turn.
  3. Repeat Row 1 until you reach your desired width for the scarf.
  4. To crochet the Jack and Sally designs, you can follow a graph pattern or use a colorwork technique. Choose the accent color for the designs and change colors accordingly.
  5. Once you've completed the design, finish off the scarf and weave in any loose ends.
