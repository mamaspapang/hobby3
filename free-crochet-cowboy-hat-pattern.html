Crochet Cowboy Hat Pattern

First things first, let's start with the cowboy hat. Here's what you'll need:

  • Medium weight yarn
  • Crochet hook (size F)
  • Yarn needle

To begin, create a magic circle and work 10 single crochet stitches into it. Pull the circle tight and join with a slip stitch to the first single crochet.

Next, chain 1 and work 2 single crochet stitches into each stitch around. Join with a slip stitch to the first single crochet. This will create a total of 20 stitches.

Continue crocheting in rounds, working single crochet stitches in each stitch around, until the hat reaches your desired height. Remember to join each round with a slip stitch.

Adding the Brim

Once the hat is the right height, it's time to add the brim. To do this, chain 1 and work 2 single crochet stitches in the first stitch. Work 1 single crochet stitch in the next stitch. Repeat this pattern around, creating increases that will make the brim flare out.

Continue working these increases for a few rounds until the brim is the desired width. Finish off by fastening the yarn and weaving in the loose ends.

Crochet Boots Pattern

Now, let's move on to the boots. Here's what you'll need:

  • Medium weight yarn
  • Crochet hook (size F)
  • Yarn needle

Start by making a foundation chain that fits around the doll's foot. Slip stitch to form a ring and work single crochet stitches into each chain stitch. Join with a slip stitch.

Next, work single crochet stitches in each stitch around for a few rounds to create the foot part of the boot. Join each round with a slip stitch.

To create the leg part of the boot, start with a chain and slip stitch it to the first stitch of the round. Continue working single crochet stitches in each stitch around, joining the rounds with a slip stitch, until the leg is the desired length.

Finish off by fastening the yarn and weaving in the loose ends.
