Step-by-Step Guide

Materials Needed:

  1. Yarn (any weight and color of your choice)
  2. Crochet hook (appropriate size for your yarn)
  3. Tapestry needle

Instructions:

  1. Start by creating a slip knot with your yarn and place it on your crochet hook.
  2. Chain a multiple of stitches in order to achieve the desired width of your shawl.
  3. Begin the first row by working single crochet stitches into each chain stitch.
  4. Continue working rows of single crochet until your shawl has reached your desired length.
  5. To add a decorative border, you can choose to crochet around the entire shawl or just along the sides.
  6. Once you've completed the border, finish off your work and weave in any loose ends.
