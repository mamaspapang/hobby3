Materials Needed

Before we begin, let's gather all the supplies you'll need:

  • Yarn in yellow, brown, and green
  • Crochet hook (size F or G)
  • Tapestry needle
  • Scissors

Instructions

1. Start by creating the center of the sunflower. Use the brown yarn and crochet a magic ring. Then, single crochet six times into the ring and pull it tight.

2. Next, switch to yellow yarn and single crochet two stitches into each stitch from the previous round. This will give you a total of twelve stitches. Close the round with a slip stitch.

3. To create the petals, chain three and double crochet five times into the first stitch. Slip stitch into the next stitch to secure the petal. Repeat this process five more times to create a total of six petals.

4. After completing all the petals, switch back to brown yarn and single crochet around the entire sunflower, including the petals. This will give a neat finishing touch.
