Getting Started

If you're new to crochet, don't worry! This pattern is beginner-friendly and comes with step-by-step instructions. Here's what you'll need to get started:

Materials:

  1. Red, white, and blue yarn - You'll need about 3 skeins of each color.
  2. Crochet hook - Size 5.0 mm works well for this project.
  3. Tapestry needle - For weaving in loose ends.
  4. Scissors - To cut the yarn.

Pattern:

  1. Start by creating a foundation chain with the red yarn.
  2. Follow the pattern chart provided by YarnHookNeedles to create the stars and stripes.
  3. Continue working on the pattern rows until you reach the desired length.
  4. Switch to the blue yarn and crochet a rectangle of stars in the corner of the blanket.
  5. Complete the border of the blanket with the white yarn.
  6. Finish off and weave in any loose ends.

By following these simple instructions, you'll have a beautiful America Flag Crochet Blanket in no time!
