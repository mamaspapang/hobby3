Materials Needed

  2. Crochet Hook: Use the appropriate hook size for your selected yarn to achieve the desired size.
  3. Safety Eyes: These will bring your otter to life – choose the size that best matches your design.
  4. Fiberfill: This will give your amigurumi a soft and cuddly texture.

Step-by-Step Process

  1. Start by crocheting the otter's body using a magic ring and single crochet stitches.
  2. Continue to follow the pattern to create the head and limbs separately.
  3. Once all the parts are complete, stuff them with fiberfill to give them a nice plump shape.
  4. Attach the head and limbs to the body using your yarn needle.
  5. Finally, attach the safety eyes and embroider the otter's mouth and nose.
