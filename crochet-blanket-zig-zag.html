How to Crochet the Zig Zag Baby Blanket

To create this beautiful blanket, you'll need some basic crochet skills and materials. Here's a step-by-step guide on how to make the Zig Zag Crochet Baby Blanket:

Materials:

  1. Medium weight yarn in your desired colors (preferably soft and baby-friendly)
  2. Size G/6 (4.00 mm) crochet hook

Step 1: Starting Chain

To begin, make a chain of multiples of 10 plus 1. This will form the foundation of your blanket.

Step 2: Setting up the Zig Zag Pattern

Row 1: In the third chain from the hook, make 2 double crochet stitches. Skip 2 chains, then make a double crochet cluster (3 double crochet stitches) in the next chain. Skip 2 chains again, and repeat the pattern of two double crochet stitches followed by a double crochet cluster across the row. Finish the row with a double crochet stitch in the last chain.

Row 2: Chain 2 and turn your work. Make 2 double crochet stitches in the first stitch. Skip the next 2 stitches and make a double crochet cluster in the next stitch. Repeat this pattern across the row, ending with a double crochet stitch in the top of the turning chain from the previous row.

Step 3: Repeating the Zig Zag Pattern

Continue repeating Row 2 until your blanket reaches the desired length. Remember to change colors as desired to create a visually appealing zigzag effect.

This Zig Zag Crochet Baby Blanket pattern gives you the flexibility to customize it based on your preferences. You can choose any color combination you like or opt for a neutral palette to make it suitable for both boys and girls.
