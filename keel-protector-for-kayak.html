Protect Your Kayak's Keel

The Keel Armor is specifically designed to safeguard your kayak's keel, preventing scratches, cracks, and other forms of damage. Easy Installation Process

Installing the Keel Armor is a breeze! It comes with a strong adhesive backing that allows you to attach it directly to your kayak's keel. Once it's in place, the Keel Armor forms a tight bond with the kayak, providing long-lasting protection.
