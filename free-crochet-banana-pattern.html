Materials Needed

Before you dive into creating this crochet banana, gather the following materials:

  1. Medium weight yarn in yellow
  2. Crochet hook size G (4.0mm)
  3. Tapestry needle
  4. Scissors

Step-by-Step Instructions

Step 1: Starting Off

To begin, create a slipknot and make a chain of six stitches. Then, join with a slip stitch to form a loop.

Step 2: Round 1

In this round, single crochet six times into the loop. This will create a circle for the base of your banana.

Step 3: Shape the Banana

Now, it's time to shape your crochet banana. Follow the pattern instructions to increase and decrease stitches, creating a curved shape that resembles a real banana. Don't forget to stuff the banana with fiberfill as you go along to give it a plump appearance.

Step 4: Finishing Touches

Once you've completed shaping the banana, finish off your work by securing the stitches and weaving in any loose ends. And there you have it ‚Äì your very own crochet banana ready to be displayed or gifted to a friend.
