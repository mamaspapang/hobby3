How to Crochet the Basket Weave Stitch

Now, let's learn how to crochet the Basket Weave Stitch. Here's a step-by-step guide:

Step 1: Foundation Chain

Start by making a foundation chain of any multiple of 4 stitches. This will ensure that the pattern comes out evenly.

Step 2: First Row

In the first row, you'll work a combination of double crochet and front post double crochet stitches. Follow the pattern below:

  1. Double crochet in the fourth chain from the hook.
  2. Double crochet in the next 3 stitches.
  3. Front post double crochet around the next 4 stitches.
  4. Repeat steps 2 and 3 until the end of the row.

Step 3: Second Row

The second row is the same as the first row. Repeat steps 2 and 3 until you reach the desired length.

By following these steps, you'll be able to create a beautiful Basket Weave Crochet Stitch pattern. Feel free to experiment with different yarn colors and weights to achieve a look that suits your style.
