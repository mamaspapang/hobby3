How to Crochet the Iris Stitch

To crochet the Iris Stitch, you'll need to follow a series of steps. Here's a step-by-step guide to help you get started:

  1. Create a foundation chain with a multiple of 4 stitches.
  2. Row 1: Start by working a single crochet (sc) into the fourth chain from the hook. Skip the next chain, then work 1 double crochet (dc) into each of the next 3 chains. *Skip the next chain, then work 1 sc into each of the next 3 chains and repeat from * to the end of the row.
  3. Row 2: Chain 3 (counts as 1 dc), turn your work. Work 1 dc into the first sc, *skip the next dc, then work 1 sc into each of the next 3 dc and repeat from * to the end of the row. Work 1 dc into the last sc.
  4. Repeat Row 2 until you reach your desired length.

Tips for Crocheting the Iris Stitch

Crocheting the Iris Stitch may seem a bit challenging at first, but with these tips, you'll be able to master it in no time:

  • Use a crochet hook that is suitable for the yarn you're using. This will ensure that your stitches are the right size and your project turns out as expected.
  • Pay attention to your tension. The Iris Stitch requires a consistent tension to achieve a uniform look.
  • Practice makes perfect! If you're struggling with the stitch, don't get discouraged. Keep practicing until you feel comfortable with it.
