3. What safety precautions should I take when kayaking?

When kayaking, it's crucial to prioritize safety. Here are a few precautions to follow:

  • Always wear a helmet and a personal flotation device.
  • Stay aware of the weather conditions and avoid kayaking during storms or strong currents.
  • Inform someone about your planned route and estimated return time.
  • Carry essential safety equipment, such as a whistle and a first-aid kit.

By following these precautions and using common sense, you can ensure a safe and enjoyable kayaking experience.
