Creating the Woodland Wolf Granny Square

The Woodland Wolf Granny Square pattern is relatively easy to follow, even for beginners. To get started, you'll need a few basic crochet supplies:

  1. Yarn: Choose your favorite colors to bring this pattern to life. Earth tones like brown, green, and gray work exceptionally well for a woodland-themed design.
  2. Crochet hook: Select an appropriate hook size based on your yarn choice. A size G/6 (4 mm) hook is usually recommended for worsted weight yarn.

Once you have your supplies ready, follow these simple steps to create your own Woodland Wolf Granny Square:

  1. Create a magic ring by wrapping the yarn around your fingers and pulling the end through the loop.
  2. Chain 3 to serve as the first double crochet (dc).
  3. Make two dc stitches into the magic ring, followed by two chain stitches.
  4. Repeat the previous step two more times, creating a total of four clusters of two dc stitches separated by two chain stitches.
  5. Close the magic ring tightly by gently pulling the loose end.
  6. Next, create the wolf image in the center by using the tapestry crochet technique and working with different color yarns.
  7. Continue with the granny square pattern around the wolf image, alternating between clusters of dc stitches and chain stitches.
  8. Repeat this pattern for the desired size of your granny square.
  9. Finish off your work and weave in any loose ends.
