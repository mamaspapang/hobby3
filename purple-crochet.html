Q: How do I care for my handmade crochet scarf?

A: To ensure the longevity of your handmade crochet scarf, it's important to handle it with care. It's recommended to hand wash the scarf using mild detergent and warm water. Avoid wringing or twisting the scarf, and instead, gently squeeze out excess water. Lay it flat to dry to maintain its shape.
