What You'll Need

Before you start, gather your supplies. For this pattern, you'll need:

  1. Crochet Hook - Size G/6 (4.00 mm)
  2. Yarn - Light pink, medium pink, and yellow
  3. Polyester Fiberfill

Step-by-Step Instructions

Now, let's get those creative juices flowing and start crocheting our adorable baby flamingoes:

  1. Head: Start with light pink yarn and make a magic ring. Work single crochet stitches into the ring and continue to increase the stitches until you have a spherical shape for the head.
  2. Body and Wings: Using medium pink yarn, crochet the body and wings separately. Stuff the body with polyester fiberfill for a plump and cuddly flamingo companion.
  3. Legs: Craft the long, slender legs using yellow yarn. Attach them securely to the body, ensuring your flamingo can stand tall.
  4. Eyes and Beak: Embroider or attach eyes and a beak to give your baby flamingo that adorable expression.
  5. Details: Feel free to add any additional details, such as feathers, to make your flamingo truly unique!
