2. Learn the C2c Crochet Basics

If you're new to C2c crochet, it's essential to familiarize yourself with the basics. You'll need to know how to make the C2c stitch, increase and decrease stitches, and change colors. Once you have a solid understanding of these techniques, you'll be ready to tackle the Spider-Man pattern.

3. Follow the Pattern

Once you have your materials and know the basics, it's time to start crocheting! Follow the Spider-Man pattern row by row, using the C2c technique.
