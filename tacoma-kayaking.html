When it comes to kayaking in Tacoma and the South Sound, the fjords and inlets are where the real magic happens. If you're looking for a more laid-back kayaking experience, head over to Ruston Way Waterfront. This scenic stretch of beach offers a perfect setting for a leisurely paddle. Take in the fresh sea breeze as you glide along the coastline, and make sure to pause and enjoy the stunning views of Mount Rainier.
