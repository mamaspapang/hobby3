Creating Your Mohair Beanie

Once you have your supplies ready, it's time to begin crocheting your mohair beanie. Follow these simple steps:

1. Make a Slip Knot

Start by making a slip knot on your crochet hook. This will be the first stitch of your beanie.

2. Chain Stitch

Next, create a chain stitch of the desired length for the circumference of your beanie. This will vary depending on the size you want.

3. Single Crochet

Now, it's time to work the body of the beanie. Begin by single crocheting into each stitch of the chain. Continue this pattern until you reach the desired height for your beanie.

4. Decrease Stitches

To finish off your beanie, you'll need to decrease the stitches gradually. This will create a tapered crown. Follow the instructions provided in the pattern to achieve the desired shape.

By following these steps and referring to the video tutorial, you'll be able to crochet your own mohair beanie with ease. The pattern is designed to be beginner-friendly, ensuring that anyone can complete the project successfully.
