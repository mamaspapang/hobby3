The first step in creating a crochet flower blanket is to gather all the necessary materials. You will need a crochet hook, yarn in various colors, and a pattern to follow. When selecting a pattern, consider the size of the blanket you want to make, as well as the types of flowers you'd like to incorporate. Some patterns focus on one type of flower, while others feature a mix of different flowers. You can choose to make a blanket with a single large flower as the centerpiece, or create a blanket covered in an assortment of smaller flowers.
