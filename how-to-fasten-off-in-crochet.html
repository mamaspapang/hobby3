The Invisible Fasten Off Technique

In this section, we'll go through step-by-step instructions on how to achieve the invisible fasten off technique. Follow along, and you'll soon master this invaluable skill.

Step 1: Preparing the Yarn

First, cut the yarn, leaving a long tail (approximately 6 inches) for weaving in later. Make sure to secure your last stitch before proceeding.

Step 2: Thread the Yarn Needle

Next, thread the yarn needle with the tail of the yarn. This will allow you to weave in the yarn later, creating a seamless finish.

Step 3: Inserting the Yarn Needle

Now, insert the yarn needle under both loops of the next stitch from front to back. Make sure to leave a small loop on the front side of the work.

Step 4: Looping the Yarn

Take the needle to the back side of the work and insert it into the corresponding stitch on the wrong side of the fabric. Loop the yarn around the needle, creating a loop on the back side.

Step 5: Finalizing the Loop

Bring the needle back up to the front side of the work, going through the loop created in step 4. Gently pull the yarn to tighten the loop.

Step 6: Securing the Yarn

Finally, insert the needle into the base of the stitch and bring it back out a short distance away. Repeat this for a few stitches, creating a secure hold for the yarn.
