Materials Needed for the keyword

  2. Cotton yarn: medium weight (approx. 100 yards)
  3. Scissors

Step-by-Step Instructions

Follow these simple steps to create your own keyword :

  1. Start by creating a slip knot with the yarn and placing it on the crochet hook.
  2. Chain 29 stitches to create the foundation row.
  3. In the second chain from the hook, work a single crochet stitch. Continue working a single crochet stitch in each chain across the row.
  4. Once you reach the end of the row, chain 1 to create the turning chain. Turn the work.
  5. Work a single crochet stitch in the first stitch of the previous row. Repeat this stitch in each stitch across the row until the end.
  6. Continue repeating step 5 until the washcloth reaches your desired size. This pattern is designed to create a washcloth that is approximately 8 inches by 8 inches.
