Creating the Zig Zag Blanket

To create the Zig Zag Blanket, you will need the following materials:

  • Yarn (choose your favorite colors!)
  • Crochet hook
  • Scissors

Once you have gathered all your materials, you can start crocheting! Here are the steps to create the Zig Zag Blanket:

Step 1: Foundation Chain

Start by making a foundation chain of the desired length. The length will depend on how big you want your blanket to be.

Step 2: First Row

In the first row, you will be alternating between single crochet and double crochet stitches to create the zig zag pattern. Follow the pattern instructions carefully to achieve the desired effect.

Step 3: Repeat Rows

Continue repeating the pattern from the first row until your blanket reaches the desired length. Make sure to finish with a row that matches the pattern of the first row.
