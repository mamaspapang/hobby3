The Joy of Amigurumi Candy Corn

1. Supplies needed:

  • Crochet hook (size F/3.75mm)
  • Yarn needle
  • Worsted weight yarn in white, orange, and yellow
  • Polyester fiberfill stuffing

2. Crocheting the candy corn:

Start by creating a magic ring with the white yarn. Then, follow the pattern instructions to crochet the base of the candy corn using the single crochet stitch. Don't forget to change colors accordingly to achieve the classic candy corn look!

Continue crocheting the middle and top sections of the candy corn, making sure to stuff it with polyester fiberfill as you go. The stuffing is essential to give your amigurumi that huggable and squishy feel.
