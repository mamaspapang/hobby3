Lazy Waves Blanket Pattern

To help you get started, here is the detailed pattern for the Lazy Waves Blanket:

Materials Needed:

  1. Yarn in your desired colors
  2. Crochet hooks (size may vary depending on your yarn)
  3. Tapestry needle

Instructions:

1. Begin by creating a foundation chain with your chosen yarn color. The length of the chain will determine the width of your blanket.

2. Once you have your foundation chain, you'll start working the lazy waves pattern. This pattern involves alternating rows of single crochet stitches and double crochet stitches.

3. Follow the pattern instructions for the desired number of rows to create the wave effect. You can experiment with different colors to make your blanket even more unique.

4. To finish off your blanket, weave in any loose ends and give it a gentle block to give it a neat and polished look.
