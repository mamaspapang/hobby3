How to Crochet Fast Blanket Mittens

To make these mittens, you'll need to gather the following materials: yarn (choose a color you love), a crochet hook, and a yarn needle. Once you have everything ready, you can start crocheting using the following steps:

1. Start with a magic ring

To begin, create a magic ring by making a loop with your yarn and pulling the working yarn through the loop. This will give you a secure starting point for your mitten.

2. Crochet the cuff

Next, crochet the cuff of the mitten by working single crochet stitches in rounds. This will create a stretchy and comfortable band that will keep the mitten in place.

3. Work on the hand and thumb

Now, it's time to crochet the hand and thumb sections of the mitten. You'll work in rounds, increasing or decreasing stitches as needed to shape the mitten. Don't forget to leave an opening for the thumb!

4. Add the finishing touches

To complete your Fast Blanket Mittens, weave in any loose ends and give them a gentle blocking. This will help the mittens retain their beautiful shape and make them even more comfortable to wear.
