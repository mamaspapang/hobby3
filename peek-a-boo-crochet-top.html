Materials and Pattern Details

To create this beautiful crochet top, you'll need the following materials:

  1. Yarn: Choose a lightweight yarn in your desired color. The pattern suggests using cotton yarn for its breathability.
  2. Crochet Hook: Select an appropriate crochet hook size based on your yarn choice. The pattern recommends using a G/6 (4.00mm) hook.
  3. Scissors: A pair of sharp scissors for cutting the yarn.
  4. Tapestry Needle: You'll need a tapestry needle for weaving in the loose ends.

Once you have gathered all the materials, you can proceed with the following steps:

Pattern Instructions

Foundation Chain

Start by making a foundation chain of the desired length to fit your bust circumference. Make sure to keep the chain loose enough for comfort.

Row 1: Single Crochet and Chain Stitch

For the first row, single crochet in the second chain from the hook and in each chain across. Then, chain one and turn.

Row 2 and Beyond: Single Crochet

Continue working single crochet stitches in each stitch across for each row until the top reaches your desired length. Remember to chain one and turn at the end of each row.

Repeat this pattern until the crochet top reaches the desired length. Once you finish, fasten off and weave in any loose ends. Your beautiful crochet top is now ready to be flaunted!
