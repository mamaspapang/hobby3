Creating Your Bend Beanie Cell Phone Case

Before we dive into the pattern, gather your favorite color of yarn and the appropriate crochet hook size. Once you have everything ready, take a look at the image below to get an idea of what your finished Bend Beanie cell phone case will look like.

Pattern Instructions

  1. Start by creating a foundation chain that matches the width of your cell phone. This will ensure a snug fit for your case.
  2. Once you have the correct number of chains, join them to form a ring using a slip stitch.
  3. Continue working in rounds, crocheting single crochet stitches all the way around the ring. Keep going until the case is the desired height.
  4. Now it's time to add some flair to your case! Feel free to experiment with different stitch patterns, color changes, or even embellishments like buttons or beads.
  5. Once you're satisfied with the overall look of your case, fasten off and weave in any loose ends.
  6. Finally, slide your cell phone into the cozy Bend Beanie case you've created. Admire how stylish and unique your phone looks!
