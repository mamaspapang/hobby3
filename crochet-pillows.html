Creating Your Perfect Decorative Pillow

Materials Needed

  1. Merino wool - The main material for this project. Ensure you have enough to complete the pattern.
  2. Crochet hook - Choose a hook suitable for the selected wool to achieve the desired gauge.
  3. Tapestry needle - To weave in loose ends and sew pieces together.
  4. Heart-shaped pillow insert - This will give your pillow the perfect shape.

Step-by-Step Instructions

Now, let's walk through the step-by-step instructions to create this decorative pillow:

  1. Start by creating a slipknot and a foundation chain of the desired length.
  2. Single crochet in the second chain from the hook, and continue single crocheting across the row.
  3. Once the row is complete, turn your work and single crochet back across the row.
  4. Continue this pattern of single crocheting rows until your work reaches the desired width.
  5. When your crochet piece is wide enough, it's time to introduce the heart pattern.

Adding the Heart Pattern

To add the heart pattern, follow these steps:

  1. Choose a row on your crochet piece where you'd like to place the first heart.
  2. Starting from the right corner, work double crochet stitches to create the top-right part of the heart.
  3. Complete the top of the heart by working a chain stitch and a slip stitch.
  4. Continue to work double crochets, chains, and slip stitches to create the remaining parts of the heart.
