Materials Needed

Before we begin, let's gather all the materials you will need for this project. Here's a list of what you'll need:

  • Crochet Hooks (Size G and F)
  • Yarn (Medium Weight, Color of Your Choice)
  • Tapestry Needle
  • Stuffing
  • Scissors

Step-by-Step Instructions

Now that we have all the materials ready, let's start creating our crochet toy cat. Follow these step-by-step instructions to make your cat come to life:

Step 1: Crochet the Head

To begin, make a magic ring with your size G crochet hook. Then, crochet 6 single crochet stitches into the ring. Pull the ring tight and slip stitch into the first single crochet to join. This will be the top of the head.

Next, crochet 2 single crochet stitches into each stitch around (12 stitches total). This will create the rounded shape of the head. Slip stitch into the first single crochet to join.

Step 2: Crochet the Body

Now, we will crochet the body of the cat. To do this, start by crocheting 2 single crochet stitches into the first stitch. Then, crochet 1 single crochet stitch into the next stitch. Repeat this pattern around (18 stitches total). Slip stitch into the first single crochet to join.

Continue crocheting 1 single crochet stitch into each stitch around for the next 10 rounds. This will create the length of the body. Once you reach the desired length, fasten off and weave in any loose ends.
