Styling Tips for Crochet Cropped Tops

2. Layer it over a Plain Camisole

If you're not comfortable showing too much skin, layering your crochet cropped top over a plain camisole can be a great option. This adds a touch of modesty to your outfit while still allowing the crochet design to shine through. It also adds a layer of interest and dimension to your look.

3. Accessorize with Statement Jewelry

To elevate your crochet cropped top outfit, don't forget to accessorize! Q: Are crochet cropped tops suitable for all body types?
