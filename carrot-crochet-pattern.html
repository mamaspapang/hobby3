Creating Your Amigurumi Carrot

Before we begin, make sure you have all the necessary materials to complete this project:

  • Crochet hook (size G/6 – 4.25mm)
  • Yarn in orange and green
  • Fiberfill stuffing
  • Yarn needle
  • Scissors

Step 1: Making the Carrot Body

Start by creating a magic ring with your orange yarn. If you're not familiar with the magic ring technique, don't worry! It's super easy. Simply wrap the yarn around your finger twice, insert your hook through the center of the ring, and pull the yarn through. Then, chain 1 to secure the ring.

Next, work 6 single crochets into the ring. To do this, insert your hook into the ring, yarn over, and pull up a loop. Then, yarn over again and pull through both loops on your hook. Repeat this process 5 more times until you have a total of 6 single crochets in the ring.

Step 2: Adding the Carrot Top

With your green yarn, create a slipknot and attach it to one of the single crochets on the last round of the carrot body. Chain 12 and skip the first chain. Then, slip stitch into the next chain. Repeat this process until you have attached the green yarn to all 6 single crochets. This will create the leafy top of the carrot.

After attaching the green yarn, cut the orange yarn, leaving a long tail. Use your yarn needle to weave in the loose ends and secure them. Your amigurumi carrot body with its leafy green top is now complete!
