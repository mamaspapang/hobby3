b) Wings and Tail

Once the head and body are complete, it's time to crochet the wings and tail. Here's how you can do it:

  1. Crochet 6 single crochet stitches into a magic ring.
  2. Increase each stitch by crocheting 2 single crochets into each of the previous stitches. This will give your parrot's wings a fuller shape.
  3. Continue crocheting in a round until the desired size of the wings is achieved.
  4. For the tail, crochet a chain of any desired length and attach it to the back of the parrot's body.
