How to Crochet the Half Double Crochet V Stitch

To create the Half Double Crochet V Stitch, you'll need to follow these simple steps:

  1. Start by making a foundation chain with an even number of stitches.
  2. Next, skip the first three chain stitches from your hook and work a half double crochet stitch in the fourth chain stitch. This will create the first V in the V stitch pattern.
  3. Then, skip the next chain stitch and work one half double crochet stitch in the next chain stitch. This will create the gap between the V stitches.
  4. Continue alternating between making V stitches and creating gaps until you reach the end of the row.
  5. For the next row, start by chaining two and turning your work.
  6. In the first gap between V stitches, work one half double crochet stitch. Then, work a V stitch in the next gap.
  7. Continue alternating between working V stitches and creating gaps until you reach the end of the row.
  8. Repeat these steps for as many rows as you want to create.
