Materials Needed

  1. Yarn: Choose your favorite colors or go with traditional Hello Kitty colors like pink and white.

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Start by crocheting the body of the Hello Kitty. Typically, it's done in rounds and starts with a magic ring.
  2. Continue by creating the head, arms, and legs separately.
  3. Once all the parts are crocheted, it's time to assemble the Hello Kitty. Sew the different parts together using the yarn needle.
  4. Fill the body and head with stuffing to give the Hello Kitty its plush and cuddly appearance.
