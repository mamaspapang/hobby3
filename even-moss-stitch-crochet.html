How to Crochet the Even Moss Stitch

Now, let's dive into the step-by-step instructions on how to crochet the Even Moss Stitch:

  1. Start by making a foundation chain with an even number of stitches.
  2. Row 1: Insert your hook into the second chain from the hook and single crochet. Skip the next chain and double crochet into the following chain. Repeat this pattern across the row, ending with a single crochet in the last chain.
  3. Row 2: Chain one and turn your work. Single crochet in the first stitch. Double crochet into the space between the single and double crochet of the previous row. Repeat this pattern across the row, ending with a single crochet in the last stitch.
  4. Repeat Row 2 for the desired length of your project.
