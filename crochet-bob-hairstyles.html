2. Messy Bun

If you're going for a more relaxed and effortless look, the messy bun is a perfect choice. It's easy to create and adds a touch of casual elegance to your overall appearance.

3. Beach Waves

Get ready for some summer vibes with beach waves! This hairstyle gives you a natural, tousled look, as if you just stepped off the sandy shores. It's a great option for a laid-back and carefree style.

4. Braided Updo

For those special occasions when you want to amp up your hairstyle game, the braided updo is a fantastic choice. It adds an element of intricate detail and elegance to your overall look.

People Also Ask

1. How do I create beach waves?

Answer: To create beach waves, you can use a curling iron or try braiding your damp hair overnight for a more natural look.

2. What face shapes suit a sleek bob with bangs?

Answer: A sleek bob with bangs looks great on almost all face shapes, but it particularly flatters oval and heart-shaped faces.

3. How do I achieve a messy bun?

Answer: To achieve a messy bun, gather your hair into a high ponytail, twist it loosely, and secure it with bobby pins. Pull out a few strands to create a relaxed and effortless look.

4. Are braided updos suitable for long hair only?

Answer: No, braided updos can be styled with both long and medium-length hair. You can always use hair extensions or accessories to create the desired effect.
