Q: Can all dogs go kayaking?

A: While most breeds can enjoy kayaking, it's important to consider your dog's temperament, size, and swimming ability. Some dogs may not feel comfortable in a kayak, while others might absolutely love it. Always introduce your dog to kayaking gradually and ensure their safety throughout the trip.

Q: How can I keep my dog safe while kayaking?

A: To keep your dog safe while kayaking, make sure they wear a properly fitted life jacket. Additionally, secure them to the kayak using a leash or harness to prevent them from jumping overboard. Familiarize your pup with the kayak before heading out on the water and always keep an eye on them to ensure their well-being.
