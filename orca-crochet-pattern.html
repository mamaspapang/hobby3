Step-by-Step Instructions

1. Start with the Basics

To begin, create a slipknot with the black yarn and slide it onto your crochet hook. Then, chain 2 stitches.

Next, work 6 single crochet stitches into the second chain from the hook. This will form the beginning of the orca's body. Tighten the ring by gently tugging on the loose yarn end.

2. Shape the Body

Now, it's time to start shaping the orca's body. Continue working in a spiral, making single crochet stitches in each stitch around. After completing each round, use a stitch marker to keep track of your progress.

Keep crocheting until the body measures approximately 4 inches in length. Remember to stuff the toy with polyester fiberfill as you go to give it a plump and cuddly feel.
