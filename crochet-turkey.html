Getting Started

The crochet turkey coaster or stuffed turkey ball pattern is a fun and beginner-friendly project. Whether you're a seasoned crocheter or just starting out, you'll have a blast making this adorable turkey-inspired creation.

To get started, gather the following materials: yarn in various colors, crochet hooks, stuffing, and a yarn needle. Once you have your materials ready, follow these simple steps:

1. Crochet the Body

Begin by creating a magic ring and working single crochets into it. Continue working in rounds, gradually increasing the number of stitches. This will give your turkey body a nice round shape.

Keep crocheting until your turkey body reaches the desired size. Depending on whether you're making a coaster or a stuffed turkey ball, adjust the size accordingly.

2. Add the Details

Once the body is complete, it's time to add the turkey's signature features. Crochet the beak, wattle, and feathers separately using different colors of yarn. You can use a combination of single crochets, double crochets, and other crochet stitches to create these details.

After completing each detail, attach them securely to the body using a yarn needle.

3. Finishing Touches

Finally, stuff the turkey body with soft filling to create a plush stuffed turkey ball or leave it as is to make a functional turkey coaster. Once you're satisfied with the stuffing, close up the opening and weave in any loose ends.
