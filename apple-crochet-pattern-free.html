In order to crochet an apple, start by making a magic ring, which will be the center of the apple. Then, crochet a chain stitch and join it with a slip stitch to form a loop. This will create the base of the apple. Next, work single crochet stitches in the round, increasing the number of stitches gradually to shape the apple.

To add texture and details to the apple, you can use different crochet techniques such as front post and back post stitches. These stitches create raised ridges on the surface of the apple, giving it a realistic look. Feel free to experiment and customize your apple according to your preferences.
