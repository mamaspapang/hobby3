Q: How long should my kayak paddle be?

A: The ideal length of your kayak paddle depends on various factors, including your height and the width of your kayak. It's recommended to choose a paddle that is 20-30 centimeters longer than the width of your kayak for optimal performance.

Q: Can I use a kayak paddle for stand-up paddleboarding?

A: While it's technically possible to use a kayak paddle for stand-up paddleboarding, it's not recommended. Stand-up paddleboarding requires a specific paddle with a longer shaft to accommodate the standing position and different strokes.
