Creating Your Own Banana Applique

Now that we've piqued your interest, let's delve into the process of creating your very own banana applique. But first, gather the following materials:

  1. A crochet hook (size depending on the desired size of your applique)
  2. Yellow yarn (choose a shade you find particularly appealing)
  3. Black embroidery floss or thread
  4. A yarn needle

Once you have everything ready, follow these steps:

Step 1: Start with the Body

Begin by forming a magic loop with your yellow yarn. If you're not familiar with magic loops, don't worry – they're quite simple. Hold the tail of the yarn with your thumb and forefinger, then wrap the yarn around them two times, making sure the tail end is at the back. Insert your crochet hook under the first loop from the front, yarn over, and pull through to create a new loop. This loop will be the starting point for your crochet stitches.

Step 2: Crochet the Body

Next, it's time to crochet the body of the banana. Insert your crochet hook through the magic loop and pull up a loop of yarn. Then, yarn over and pull through both loops on your hook. Repeat this process until you have six single crochet stitches in total.

Once you complete the sixth single crochet stitch, pull the tail end of the yarn to close the magic loop. This will create a tight round shape for the body of the banana. We're making progress!

Step 3: Shape the Sides

To give your banana applique a more realistic appearance, it's essential to shape the sides. Start by crocheting two regular single crochet stitches. Then, work two half-double crochet stitches into the next stitch.

Now, repeat the pattern by crocheting two regular single crochet stitches followed by two half-double crochet stitches. Continue this process until you reach the end of the round.

Step 4: Add the Peel Lines

The peel lines are what truly bring your banana to life. Using your black embroidery floss or thread, embroider three diagonal lines on the body of the banana. These lines should resemble the natural curves of a real banana peel.

And voila! Your adorable and squishy banana applique is complete! Get creative and experiment with different yarn colors or sizes for an even more personalized touch.
