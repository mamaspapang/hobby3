Q: How do I clean crochet coasters?

A: Cleaning crochet coasters is quite simple. You can gently hand wash them using mild detergent and warm water. Rinse them thoroughly and lay them flat to dry. Avoid using bleach or harsh chemicals, as they can damage the delicate fibers.

Q: Can I use these crochet coasters outdoors?

A: While crochet coasters are primarily designed for indoor use, you can certainly use them outdoors on a patio or during a picnic. However, keep in mind that prolonged exposure to sunlight and moisture may cause the colors to fade or the coasters to lose their shape over time. It's best to bring them indoors when not in use for extended durability.
