Materials Needed

Before we begin, let's gather all the materials you'll need:

  1. Crochet hook (size G)
  2. Yarn (worsted weight)
  3. Scissors

Step-by-Step Instructions

Now, let's get started with the crochet process. Follow these step-by-step instructions:

Step 1: Starting Chain

To begin, make a starting chain of 80 stitches using your crochet hook and yarn. This will serve as the base for your boho hood.

Step 2: First Row

Once you have your starting chain, work a double crochet into the third chain from the hook. Then, continue working double crochets across the entire row. This will form the foundation for your hood.

Step 3: Second Row

For the second row, chain 2 (acts as the first double crochet), and then work double crochets across the row just like in the previous row. Repeat this row until your hood reaches the desired length.

Step 4: Adding a Border

Once your hood has reached the desired length, it's time to add a border for some extra flair. You can choose a contrasting color for the border to make it stand out. Simply work a row of single crochet stitches along the edge of the hood.

And that's it! You now have a beautiful and stylish Easy Basic Boho Hood. Let's move on to discussing the benefits and advantages of this crochet pattern.
