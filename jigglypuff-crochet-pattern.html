    3. Black yarn

    5. Yarn needle
    6. Stuffing

    Begin by crocheting the body of Jigglypuff. Use the pink yarn and follow the specified stitch pattern to create the desired shape. Make sure to stuff the body lightly as you go for a plush finish.

    Next, it's time to bring Jigglypuff's face to life. Use the white yarn to crochet the oval-shaped eyes and the smirk. Then, use the black yarn to embroider the pupils and mouth. Don't forget to attach the facial features securely to the body.

    Jigglypuff's iconic tuft of hair is what makes it so recognizable. Crochet the hair using the pink yarn, following the specified instructions. Attach the hair to the top of Jigglypuff's head, and voila! Your Jigglypuff now has its signature hairstyle.
