Getting Started

Before we begin, gather all the necessary materials for this project. You'll need some yarn in your favorite color, a crochet hook, and a pair of scissors. Make sure you have a comfortable and well-lit space to work in. Now, let's get started!

Step 1: Foundation Chain

To begin, create a foundation chain of the desired length. The foundation chain will determine the width of your project. Use the crochet hook to make a slip knot, then chain the required number of stitches. This is the first row of your project.

Step 2: Row 1

In the first row, you'll start by working into the second chain from the hook. Insert the hook into the second chain, yarn over, and pull up a loop. You should have two loops on the hook. Yarn over again and pull through both loops. This is a single crochet stitch.

Continue working single crochet stitches into each chain across the row. When you reach the end, chain one and turn your work.
