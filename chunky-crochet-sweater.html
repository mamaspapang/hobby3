Getting Started

Before you begin, make sure you have all the necessary supplies. You'll need chunky weight yarn, a crochet hook, and a tapestry needle. The great thing about this pattern is that you can choose any color you like. So, pick your favorite and let's dive in!

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Start by creating a foundation chain of the desired length. This will be the width of your sweater.
  2. Once you have your foundation chain, start working in rows to create the body of the sweater. You'll be using a combination of stitches, such as single crochet, double crochet, and half-double crochet, to achieve the desired texture.
  3. Continue working in rows, following the pattern instructions, until you reach your desired length for the body of the sweater.
  4. Next, you'll move on to creating the sleeves. Again, you'll be working in rows and using a combination of stitches to achieve the desired look.
  5. Once you've completed both sleeves, it's time to sew everything together. Use a tapestry needle and the same yarn you used for the sweater to seam the sides and attach the sleeves.
