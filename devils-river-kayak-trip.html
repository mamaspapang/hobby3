Q: What is the best time to go on a Devils River kayak trip?

A: The best time to go on a Devils River kayak trip is during the spring or fall seasons when the weather is more pleasant. Summer months can be extremely hot, while winter may bring unpredictable water levels.

Q: Can I camp along the Devils River?

A: Yes, camping is allowed along the riverbanks. There are designated camping areas where you can set up camp and enjoy the beauty of the surroundings.

Q: Is the Devils River suitable for beginners?

A: The Devils River is more suitable for intermediate or experienced kayakers due to its rapids and swift currents. Beginners are recommended to gain some kayaking experience before attempting this adventure.

Q: Are there any restrictions or permits required for the Devils River kayak trip?
