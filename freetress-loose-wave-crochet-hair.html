Why Choose Freetress Deep Wave Hair?

1. Natural Look: Freetress Deep Wave hair mimics the look and feel of natural wavy hair. It blends seamlessly with your own hair, giving you a natural and realistic appearance.

2. Versatility: With Freetress Deep Wave hair, you can experiment with different styles and looks. You can wear it loose for a carefree and bohemian vibe, or you can style it up for a more elegant and sophisticated look.

3. Low Maintenance: Crochet hairstyles using Freetress Deep Wave hair are relatively low maintenance. The hair is easy to care for and requires minimal styling. Simply spritz some water or leave-in conditioner to refresh the waves, and you're good to go.

How to Install Freetress Deep Wave Hair?

1. Prepare Your Hair: Start by thoroughly washing and conditioning your natural hair. Make sure it's free from any tangles or knots. If desired, you can also braid your hair or cornrow it for a neater look.

2. Create the Base: To install the Freetress Deep Wave hair, you'll need a crochet latch hook and a crochet braid pattern. Begin by creating a thin cornrow braid going around your head, leaving a small section of hair at the front for a natural-looking hairline.

3. Attach the Hair: Take a section of the Freetress Deep Wave hair and fold it in half. Insert the latch hook under a cornrow and open the latch. Hook the folded hair onto the latch and close it securely. Pull the latch back through the cornrow, bringing the hair loop through to the other side.

4. Secure the Hair: Once the hair is pulled through, make a small knot to secure it in place. Repeat this process across the cornrows until all of your hair is covered with the Freetress Deep Wave hair.

3. Easy to Install: With the crochet method, installing Freetress Deep Wave hair is quick and easy. You can either do it yourself or have a stylist assist you. It doesn't require any heat or glue, which makes it a safer option for your hair.

4. Affordable: Freetress Deep Wave hair is an affordable option compared to other types of hair extensions or weaving methods. You can achieve a stunning hairstyle without breaking the bank.

Q: How long does Freetress Deep Wave hair last?
