Materials Needed

  1. Size K (6.5 mm) crochet hook
  2. Worsted weight yarn
  3. Yarn needle

Instructions

  1. Start by chaining 201 stitches.
  2. Work in single crochet stitches until the scarf measures your desired length.
  3. Join the ends of the scarf to create the infinity shape.
  4. Weave in any loose ends.
