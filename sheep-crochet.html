Crochet Sheep Applique - Free Crochet Pattern - Maria's Blue Crayon is a charming craft project that you can easily create. With just a few materials and some basic crochet skills, you'll be able to make adorable sheep appliques to embellish your belongings. To get started, gather the following materials:

Materials

  1. Worsted weight yarn in white and black
  2. Crochet hook (size G/4.25mm)
  3. Yarn needle
  4. Scissors

Step 1: Crocheting the Head

Begin by crocheting the head of the sheep. Use the white yarn and create a magic ring. Then, work 6 single crochet stitches into the ring and pull it tight. Continue by working 2 single crochet stitches into each stitch around, resulting in 12 stitches. This will form the head.

Next, crochet 1 single crochet stitch into the next stitch and 2 single crochet stitches into the following stitch. Repeat this pattern until you reach the end of the round. You should have a total of 18 stitches. In the next round, crochet 1 single crochet stitch into each stitch around.

Step 2: Adding the Facial Features

Once the head is complete, it's time to add the facial features. Thread the black yarn into the yarn needle and embroider two small eyes using satin stitches. For the nose, create a French knot in black yarn just below the eyes. Finally, sew a smiling mouth using black yarn and straight stitches.

For the ears, chain 9 stitches using the white yarn. Then, slip stitch into the second chain from the hook and single crochet into the next 7 chains. Fasten off and sew the ears onto the head, above the eyes and slightly angled outwards.

Step 3: Attaching the Sheep Applique

To attach the sheep applique to your desired item, thread the yarn needle with the same color as the item you're attaching it to. Place the sheep where you want it and sew it on using small stitches along the body and ears. Make sure to secure the ends of the yarn at the back of the applique.
