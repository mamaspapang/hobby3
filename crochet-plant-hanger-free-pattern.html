How to Crochet a Plant Hanger

To get started on your crochet plant hanger, you'll need the following materials:

Materials:

  1. A crochet hook (size may vary depending on the thickness of the yarn you choose)
  2. Yarn in your preferred color
  3. A small wooden or metal ring
  4. Scissors and a yarn needle

Once you have gathered your materials, you can follow these step-by-step instructions:

  1. Begin by creating a slip knot with your yarn and securing it to the hook.
  2. Next, create a chain of stitches to form the base of the hanger. The length of your chain will determine the size of your hanger.
  3. Once you have reached the desired length of the chain, join the ends together to form a circle.
  4. Now, you can start crocheting the body of the hanger using your preferred stitch. You can choose from various stitches like single crochet, half double crochet, or double crochet.
  5. Continue crocheting in rounds until you reach the desired length for your plant hanger.
  6. Next, create a loop at the top of the hanger to hang it from the wooden or metal ring.
  7. Finally, fasten off the yarn and weave in any loose ends.
