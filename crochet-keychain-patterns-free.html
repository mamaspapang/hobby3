Materials Needed

  1. Crochet hook (size may vary depending on your preference)
  2. Yarn in your desired color
  3. Keychain ring or lobster claw clasp
  4. Scissors

Step 1: Chain Stitch

Start by creating a slipknot and chaining the desired number of stitches. This will depend on the size of your keychain and the thickness of your yarn. Remember to leave a long tail at the beginning for later use.

Step 2: Single Crochet

Once you have completed the chain stitches, insert your crochet hook into the second chain from the hook. Yarn over and pull through both loops on the hook, creating a single crochet stitch. Continue single crocheting in each chain stitch until you reach the end.

Step 3: Shape and Design
