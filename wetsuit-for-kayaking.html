Q: What size wetsuit should I buy?

A: It is crucial to choose the right size wetsuit to ensure a proper fit. Measure your chest, waist, and inseam, and refer to the wetsuit manufacturer's sizing chart for guidance. It's also a good idea to try on the wetsuit before making a purchase to ensure it fits comfortably.

Q: Can I use a wetsuit for other water activities?

A: Yes, you can use a wetsuit for various water activities such as surfing, paddleboarding, and snorkeling. However, keep in mind that different water activities may require specific wetsuits with different thicknesses and features. Consider your specific needs and check the wetsuit's suitability for your intended activity.
