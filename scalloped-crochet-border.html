Step 1: Gather the necessary supplies

Before getting started, make sure you have the following tools handy: a pair of sharp scissors, a ruler, a scoring tool, and some adhesive. Additionally, choose the desired patterned paper to create the scalloped border.

Step 2: Measure and cut the paper

Take the patterned paper and measure the desired width and length for your scalloped border. Use a ruler and mark the measurements lightly with a pencil. Once done, use your scissors to carefully cut along the marked lines.

Step 3: Score and fold the paper

Using the scoring tool, create evenly spaced score lines along the length of the cut paper. Make sure the lines are parallel to the scalloped edge. Once the scores are done, carefully fold along the lines to create accordion-like folds.

Step 4: Glue the scalloped border together

Apply adhesive on one end of the folded border and join it with the other end to form a circle. Be careful to align the scallops properly to maintain the neatness of the design. Hold the ends together firmly until the adhesive sets.
