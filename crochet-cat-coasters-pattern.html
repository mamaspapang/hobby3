Step by Step Instructions

  1. Start by chaining a few stitches to form the base of your coaster.
  2. Next, follow the pattern instructions to create the adorable cat face.
  3. Once you're done with the face, continue crocheting the body and tail of the coaster.
  4. After completing the main body, don't forget to add a cute little nose and whiskers!
  5. Finally, sew up the sides and stuff the coaster lightly to give it a plump look.
