Before you start crocheting your axolotl, gather all the required materials. You will need yarn in various colors, a crochet hook, stuffing, and safety eyes. Once you have everything ready, you can begin the fun and rewarding process of crocheting your axolotl.

Step 1: Start with the Body

Begin by crocheting the body of the axolotl. Follow the pattern instructions carefully, making sure to count your stitches as you go. Create the desired shape of the body using increases and decreases. Don't forget to leave an opening for stuffing later.

Step 2: Make the Legs and Tail
