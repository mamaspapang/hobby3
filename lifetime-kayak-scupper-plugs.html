Why Do You Need Kayak Scupper Plugs?

Kayaks come equipped with scupper holes, which are designed to drain water that enters the kayak. While these holes are essential for preventing water buildup in the kayak, they can sometimes lead to water entering the kayak, especially during rough waters or heavy rainfall.

That's where the Lifetime Kayak Scupper Plug Sets come in. By plugging the scupper holes with these specially designed plugs, you can prevent water from entering your kayak, keeping you dry and comfortable throughout your kayaking adventure.
