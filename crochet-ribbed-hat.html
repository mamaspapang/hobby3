A Classic Ribbed Design

The ribbed design adds an extra layer of texture and style to the beanie. It gives the hat a snug fit, making it perfect for those cold and windy days. The ribbed pattern is achieved by alternating between front post double crochet (fpdc) and back post double crochet (bpdc), creating a beautiful ribbed effect.

Materials You'll Need

  1. Size G (4.0 mm) crochet hook
  2. Worsted weight yarn in blue (or your desired color)
  3. Yarn needle
  4. Scissors

Make sure you have all your materials ready before you begin. Once you have everything, we can move on to the next steps.

Step-by-Step Instructions

1. Start by creating a slip knot and chaining 64 stitches.

2. Join the last chain to the first chain with a slip stitch to create a ring. Be careful not to twist your chain.

3. Chain 2 (counts as the first stitch), and then make one double crochet (dc) in each chain around. Join with a slip stitch to the top of the first chain 2.

4. Chain 2, and then make one fpdc in the next stitch. *Make one bpdc in the next stitch, followed by one fpdc in the next stitch.* Repeat from * to * around. Join with a slip stitch to the top of the first chain 2.

5. Repeat step 4 until your hat measures around 8 inches in length. You can adjust the length based on your preference.

6. To finish off, cut the yarn, leaving a long tail. Thread the yarn through the yarn needle, and weave it through the stitches to close the top of the hat. Secure and weave in any loose ends.

Q: How long does it typically take to crochet this hat?

A: The time it takes to complete the hat will vary depending on your crochet speed and experience level. On average, it can take around 3-4 hours to finish.

Q: Can I make this hat in different sizes?

A: Absolutely! To adjust the size of the hat, you can increase or decrease the number of starting chains. Additionally, you can make the hat taller or shorter by adding or reducing the number of rounds.
