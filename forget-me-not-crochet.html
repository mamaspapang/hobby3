Getting Started

To begin your crochet forget-me-not, gather the following materials:

  1. A size G crochet hook.
  2. Worsted weight yarn in the color of your choice.
  3. Scissors.
  4. A yarn needle.

Step-by-Step Instructions

1. Start with a Magic Ring

To create the center of your crochet forget-me-not, begin by making a magic ring. To do this, wrap the yarn around your fingers twice, leaving a long tail. Gently remove the yarn from your fingers, and insert your hook into the ring. Pull up a loop and chain 1 to secure the ring. You're now ready to move on to the next step!

2. Crochet the Petals

To create the petals of your forget-me-not, start by working 8 single crochets into the magic ring. After completing the 8th single crochet, slip stitch into the first single crochet to join the round. This will form your first petal. Repeat this process for a total of 5 petals. You now have the foundation of your forget-me-not!

3. Finalize Your Flower

To complete your crochet forget-me-not, cut the yarn, leaving a long tail. Thread the yarn tail through a yarn needle and weave it through the back loops of each stitch in the final round. Pull the yarn tight to cinch the flower center closed. Finally, securely fasten the yarn and trim any excess. Your beautiful crochet forget-me-not is now complete!
